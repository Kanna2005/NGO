Choose a role that aligns with your passion & expertise

Teaching Assistant

Support special educators in inclusive classrooms, assist with IEP activities, and mentor children with disabilities in foundational literacy.

Assistive Tech Mentor

Help children and teachers use screen readers, speech-to-text tools, and adaptive devices. Basic tech fluency required.

Remote / On-site

Therapy Support Volunteer
