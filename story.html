Our Evolution

The Bharat Foundation Story

We started with a simple belief: every child deserves the chance to dream, learn, and thrive. Three decades later, that belief has become a movement reaching over 7 million young minds.
XYZ, Founder
1995

The Humble Beginning

Bharat Foundation was founded with a single classroom in a small village in Maharashtra, serving 35 children with basic literacy and nutrition support. What started as a grassroots effort quickly gained momentum as communities embraced the vision.

2005

Scaling Nationwide

Expanded operations to 12 states, launching the "Hamara Gaon" community-led education model. Recognized by the Ministry of Education for innovative approaches to foundational learning.

2015

Inclusive Education (ENABLE)

Launched ENABLE - India's first large-scale inclusive education program for children with disabilities, reaching over 500,000 children across 18 states with assistive technology and trained special educators.

2024

Today & Beyond

7M+ children impacted, 26 states, 15K+ villages, and partnerships with leading global organizations. Our digital initiatives are now powered by AI and reach remote corners of the country.
