Program Overview

What is ENABLE?

Physical Accessibility

Ramps, accessible washrooms, adapted furniture, and barrier-free learning environments in 5,000+ partner schools.

Learning Support

Individualized Education Plans (IEPs), resource rooms, and remedial support for children with intellectual disabilities.

Visual & Hearing Support

Braille materials, audio books, hearing aids, and sign language instruction for deaf and hard-of-hearing students.

Assistive Technology

Screen readers, speech-to-text software, adaptive keyboards, and custom learning apps for diverse needs.

🌟 Our Commitment to Inclusion

ENABLE works on a twin-track approach: mainstreaming children with disabilities into regular schools with support, while also running specialized resource centers. We've trained over 15,000 teachers in inclusive pedagogy, and 78% of our enrolled children have shown significant improvement in learning outcomes within 18 months.

Support Services

How We Help

  • Early identification and intervention camps in rural communities
  • Home-based learning support for children with severe disabilities
  • Parent counseling and support groups for caregivers
  • Therapy services (occupational, speech, physiotherapy) at our centers
  • Vocational training and job placement for youth with disabilities
  • Awareness campaigns to combat stigma and promote inclusion

Teacher Training

Comprehensive 5-day certification program on inclusive teaching strategies, behavior management, and assistive technology use. Over 15,000 teachers trained to date.

Community Engagement

Village-level disability resource groups, awareness rallies, and home visits to identify out-of-school children with disabilities.
