📖 Our Flagship: Pratham-Jameel Second Chance Program & Inclusive Education (ENABLE)

Bharat Foundation's journey began in 1995 with a focus on early childhood education. Today we run Hamara Gaon (community-led learning), ENABLE for children with disabilities, and vocational skilling hubs that have inspired systemic reforms across India.

Vocational Training & Digital Initiatives: AI-based literacy, mobile classrooms, and teacher training institutes.
