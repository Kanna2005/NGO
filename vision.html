🇮🇳 Our North Star

A Vision for Every Child, Every Dream

We envision an India where every child, regardless of background or ability, has access to quality education, skills, and opportunities to thrive.
Our Mission

To transform lives through innovative, scalable, and community-driven education and skilling programs that break the cycle of poverty and empower India's next generation.

Access Equity Quality
Our Vision 2030

A Bharat where 25 million children and youth are equipped with foundational literacy, digital skills, and livelihood opportunities — creating a ripple effect of prosperity across every village and urban slum.

Our Roadmap

Strategic Goals for 2030

01

Universal Literacy

Ensure foundational literacy and numeracy for 15 million children across 500,000 villages by 2028.

02

Inclusive Education

Scale ENABLE program to reach 2 million children with disabilities with assistive technology and trained educators.

03

Youth Livelihoods

Train 5 million youth in vocational and digital skills, connecting them to sustainable employment.

04

Digital Equity

Bridge the digital divide through AI-powered learning platforms and mobile classrooms in remote areas.

Data-Driven Impact

Real-time monitoring, AI-driven insights, and rigorous evaluation ensure every rupee creates maximum impact. Our Learning Management System tracks progress across 15,000+ villages.

  • AI-powered adaptive learning
  • Transparent impact dashboards

Community-Led Models

Hamara Gaon — our flagship community ownership model empowers local leaders, parents, and teachers to drive change from within.

  • 25,000+ community volunteers
  • 500+ parent-teacher committees
  • Sustainable beyond funding
