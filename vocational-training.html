🎯 Our Training Model

We follow a 70% practical + 30% theory approach with real-world simulations. Each trainee receives a toolkit, safety equipment, and 6 months of post-placement support. Our placement cell works with 1,200+ corporate partners to ensure sustainable employment with minimum wage guarantee.

Courses & Duration

Program Structure

  • Foundation Course: 3 months (Basic skills & soft skills)
  • Advanced Diploma: 6 months (Specialization in chosen trade)
  • On-Job Training: 2 months internship with stipend
  • Entrepreneurship Track: Business planning & micro-loan support
